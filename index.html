About Me

我叫张怡雯，朋友叫我 Yina。现在在 UIUC 念金融工程硕士，本科在 NYU 学数学，顺带修了数据科学。对量化研究、时间序列、机器学习这些方向都有兴趣，大方向上就是想搞清楚市场里藏着什么规律。

I'm Yiwen (Yina) Zhang, studying MS Financial Engineering at UIUC. I did my undergrad in Mathematics at NYU with a minor in Data Science. I'm interested in quant research, time series, and machine learning — basically using math to make sense of things, then writing code to see if I'm right.

实习做过因子研究和策略回测，从数据清洗到引擎搭建都经历过，踩了不少坑。现在在备考 CFA 和 FRM，也参与过一个期权定价方向的研究课题。

Internships gave me a chance to work in real production environments — I've built factor models and backtest engines from scratch, and made plenty of mistakes along the way. Currently studying for CFA and FRM, and I was part of a research project on options pricing.

对模型稳健性比较执着——回测漂亮没什么用，真正上了实盘还能跑才算本事。更想搞懂策略为什么失效，而不只是记录它什么时候赚了钱。

I'm genuinely curious about model robustness. A strategy that looks great in backtests but breaks in live markets doesn't mean much — I want to understand why things fail, not just when they work.
